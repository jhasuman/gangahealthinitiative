About Us

Founded in Patna, Bihar, our organization is committed to creating a sustainable healthcare ecosystem by collaborating with various stakeholders, including hospitals, doctors, NGOs, and government bodies. Our goal is to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and underserved communities.

Our Mission

To make quality healthcare accessible and affordable for 500 million Indians by leveraging technology, partnerships, and community engagement.

Our Stakeholders

Hospitals

Collaborating with hospitals to provide affordable treatments and services.

Doctors

Engaging with doctors to deliver quality care to underserved communities.

NGOs

Partnering with NGOs to reach remote areas and marginalized groups.

Government

Working with government bodies to implement healthcare policies effectively.
